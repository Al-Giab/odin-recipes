Best Chocolate chip Cookies

Description

Soft, warm, and utterly irresistible, these chocolate chip cookies are a timeless treat that evoke feelings of comfort and indulgence. As they emerge from the oven, their golden-brown exteriors crackle with anticipation, releasing a tantalizing aroma that fills the air with the promise of sweet satisfaction. With each bite, the senses are awakened by the perfect balance of chewy dough and melty chocolate chips, creating a symphony of flavors that dance on the palate. Whether enjoyed fresh out of the oven or savored with a glass of cold milk, chocolate chip cookies are a nostalgic delight that never fails to delight both young and old alike.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup softened butter
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons hot water
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups-all purpose flour
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts

Steps

  1. Gather your ingredients, making sure your butter is softened, and your eggs are room temperature.
  2. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)
  3. Beat butter, white sugar, and brown sugar with an electric mixer in a large bowl until smooth.
  4. Beat in eggs, one at a time, then stir in vanilla
  5. Dissolve baking soda in hot water. Add to batter along with salt.
  6. Stir in flour, chocolate chips, and walnuts.
  7. Drop spoonfuls of dough 2 inches apart onto ungreased baking sheets
  8. Bake in the preheated oven until edges are nicely browned, about 10 minutes.
  9. Cool on the baking sheets briefly before removing to a wire rack to cool completely
  10. Store in an airtight container or serve immediately and enjoy!
